# Client Contract Notes

Before selling or customizing this system for a client, use a written agreement.

## Important Terms To Cover

- Scope of work
- Price
- Payment schedule
- Delivery timeline
- Revision limits
- Hosting responsibility
- Maintenance responsibility
- Ownership of custom client content
- Ownership of original I AM THE ONE™ / WOLF OS™ codebase
- License terms for client use
- No resale or copying without permission
- Support limits
- Refund policy
- Credentials handoff process

## Simple Position

The client can pay for a customized deployment or implementation.

Andrew Wolverton keeps ownership of the original system, framework, reusable code, branding, and WOLF OS™ structure unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.
